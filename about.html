About me

Lifelong experience in the medical field.

Medical oncologist with 30+ years affiliated with the Erasmus University Medical Center & Cancer Institute, holding a full professorship in Genitourinary Oncology.

Over more than three decades I have been deeply involved in clinical cancer trial design and conduct — across germ-cell, urothelial, renal and prostate cancers, as well as gynecological and breast cancer research.

Alongside this, I have built 20 years of extensive experience chairing and serving on Data & Safety Monitoring Committees for industry studies in a broad variety of solid cancers.

Key roles have included:

  • Former chairman of the EORTC Genito-Urinary Group's Chemotherapy Committee
  • Founding and current chairman of the Dutch Uro-Oncology Study Group
  • Member of the Academy of Europe
